Evidence from the Field

SPPB is not just a clinical metric; it is a proven predictor of workforce sustainability.

Employment Retention

Berner et al. (2018) - Rheumatoid Arthritis Patients (18-65y)

Key Finding: High SPPB scores are a significant predictor of maintaining employment with an Odds Ratio of 1.71.

Impact of Intervention

FRAILMERIT Study (Spain) - Community Intervention

Key Finding: A multicomponent intervention led to an 11.9% absolute risk reduction in functional decline. A 1-point change is clinically meaningful.
