SPPB Test Protocol

Test order: Balance → Gait Speed → Chair Stand  |  Total time: ~10–15 min manually / <3 min with AndanteFit

andantefit.info

1   Balance Test

Setup

  • Stand near wall. Shoes on.
  • Demonstrate each stance before timing.
  • Arms hang freely; do not hold participant.

Procedure

  • Side-by-side: Medial borders touching. Hold 10 s. If <10 s → score 0, stop.
  • Semi-tandem: Side of foot against big toe. Hold 10 s. If <10 s → score 1, stop.
  • Full tandem: Heel against toes. Time up to 10 s.
0Side-by-side <10 s
1Side-by-side ≥10 s; semi-tandem <10 s
2Semi-tandem ≥10 s; full tandem <3 s
3Full tandem 3–9.99 s
4Full tandem ≥10 s
2   Gait Speed Test

Setup

  • Mark 4-meter course on flat surface.
  • Participant may use usual walking aid.
  • Start from static position behind start line.

Procedure

  • "Walk at your usual pace."
  • Start timing when participant begins walking.
  • Stop when leading foot crosses end line.
  • Two trials; use faster time.
0Unable to complete
1≥8.70 s (<0.46 m/s)
26.21–8.70 s (0.46–0.64 m/s)
34.82–6.20 s (0.64–0.83 m/s)
4<4.82 s (>0.83 m/s)
3   Chair Stand Test

Setup

  • Firm chair ~43–45 cm seat height, no armrests.
  • Arms folded across chest; feet flat on floor.
  • Trial rise (no arms) to verify ability.

Procedure

  • "Rise fully and sit down 5 times, as quickly as you safely can."
  • Start timing on first rise.
  • Stop on full stand of 5th rise.
  • Score 0 if arms used or >60 s.
